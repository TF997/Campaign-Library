1. Origin:

  • Where is your character originally from?
    • Are they a native of Ravenford, or did they migrate here? If so, from where and why?

2. Family and Early Life:

  • What is known about your character’s family?
    • Are they still in contact with their family? What is/was their relationship like?
    • Did any family members play a significant role in shaping who they are today?

3. Significant Events:

  • What are one or two defining moments in your character’s past that set them on the path to becoming an adventurer?
    • How did these events influence their beliefs, skills, or goals?

4. Profession and Skills:

  • What training or education has your character received?
    • Do they have a particular set of skills or a profession they practiced before becoming an adventurer?
    • How did they acquire these skills?

5. Connection to Ravenford:

  • What ties does your character have to Ravenford?
    • Do they have any significant relationships, debts, or affiliations within the town?
    • How do they feel about the mystical and historical significance of Ravenford?

6. Personal Motivation:

  • Why is your character drawn to adventuring?
    • Are they seeking wealth, knowledge, power, redemption, or perhaps something more personal?
    • How does this motivation influence their actions and choices?

7. Attitudes and Beliefs:

  • What are your character’s key beliefs and values?
    • How do they view the balance between order and chaos, especially in a place influenced by both?
    • Are there any particular laws or traditions in Ravenford or Talonmere that they strongly support or oppose?

8. Friends and Foes:

  • Does your character have any allies or enemies in Ravenford or elsewhere in Talonmere?
    • How did these relationships come to be?
    • What impact have these relationships had on their life?

9. Personal Secrets:

  • What is something secretive or unknown about your character that could affect their journey?
    • Does your character have any hidden agendas or unresolved personal quests?

10. Future Aspirations:

  • What does your character hope to achieve through their adventures?
    • Where do they see themselves after their adventures in Talonmere are over?
